Zion In Christ Orphanage Home

Following the vision birthed passion of Evangelist Bilhatu Vou Dung, Zion In Christ Orphanage Home was established to provide care to vulnerable children, particularly orphans who lost their parents in terrorist and bandit attacks. Our mission is to offer these children a home and family life opportunity under the loving Lordship of Jesus Christ and also extends the redemptive love of Christ to widows and less privilege persons.

Registration Number: RC 98841

Established: January, 2020.

OUR VISION

To change the lives of orphans by helping them have a future through God's love, believing that God will provide them with family life once again, regardless of their tribe, race, nationality, or background.

OUR MISSION

To nurture orphans in a Christ-centered home, equipping them through activities and programs that develop their potentials and positioning them in society to maximize their impact.

Zion in Christ Orphanage is located at Zion Street, Adjacent Redeem Christian Church of God, Bagado, Kamazou, Kaduna, Kaduna State, Nigeria.
